What is Engineering Physics?

Engineering Physics is one of the most unique and competitive programs. Combining aspects of software, electrical and mechanical engineering, there is a heavy emphasis on applicable design. The program is designed to integrate these aspects of engineering and provides avenues for students to develop their own devices, products, and on the grander scale, careers!
